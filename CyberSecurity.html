Cyber security:

Introduction:

  • Computer science is making constant incredible breakthroughs in the world of cyber security
  • This benefits society greatly as it lowers risks involved in using digital services, and contributes to the economy by existing as a constantly growing industry

Protection of data:

  • Improvement to cyber security allows businesses that hold sensitive data, such as banks or other financial services, to better safeguard user data.
  • This helps to prevent fraud, and therefore reduce risks for the clients of these services.

Economic Contribution:

  • As cyber security exists as a constantly growing industry, it leads to more and more demand for professionals.
  • This leads to job creation and contributing to the global economy through software development, consulting and hardware production.

Risk mitigation:

  • As well as dealing with problems that have already arisen, cyber security can identify problems before they happen.
  • By identifying problems before they happen, we can conserve resources and money that would have had to go into solving the problem
  • Often the easiest time to fix a problem is before it happens, making identifying problems early very important
